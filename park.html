Tour The Play Park

The Colletta Atlanta Park Resort Center offers a variety of activities for all ages, including hiking, biking, canoeing, paddleboarding, fishing, rock climbing, and zip-lining. There's also an eco-friendly playground and a water park for families and children to enjoy.

An adventure-filled world awaits.
