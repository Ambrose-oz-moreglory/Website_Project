Colletta Atlanta

Explore the wonders of Colletta Atlanta, nature's unparalleled gift, where visitors can immerse themselves in a world of beauty. This premier attraction showcases a vibrant marketplace featuring top-selling local produce and plants, alongside a charming petting zoo, a welcoming cafe, a playful park, and serene walking paths. Colletta also presents a unique dining experience with its "For the Table" option, designed to enhance the joy of family-style dining. Available every morning to evening in addition to the extensive menu, it celebrates the essence of shared meals and diverse cuisines.
