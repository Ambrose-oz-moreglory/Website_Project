About Colletta Atlanta

Colletta Atlanta is a premier destination located in the vibrant heart of the city, offering a rich blend of leisure, shopping, and culinary experiences. It features Colletta Park, a tranquil green space with winding trails, beautiful gardens, and peaceful water features, perfect for both relaxation and outdoor adventures.

The shopping center within Colletta Atlanta boasts a wide range of shops from high-end boutiques to local artisans, catering to shoppers in search of both international brands and unique items.

Mission and Vision

At Colletta Atlanta, we are driven by a passion to enrich the lives of our visitors through exceptional experiences. Our mission is to create a community hub where the beauty of nature, the excitement of shopping, and the joy of dining converge in harmony.
